How can I make my flowers bloom longer?

To extend the blooming life of your flowers, keep them in a cool area, away from direct sunlight and heat sources. Trim dead petals regularly, change the water frequently, and use flower food to help nourish them.

How do I choose the right flowers for my home?

Consider the lighting conditions of your space. Some flowers thrive in bright, direct sunlight, while others prefer low light. Be sure to choose flowers that match the environment in your home.

How often should I water my flowers?

Water your flowers when the soil feels dry to the touch. Avoid over-watering as this can lead to root rot. Each flower variety may have different watering needs, so it's always best to check the specific care instructions.
